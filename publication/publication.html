Explainable AI for Predictive Analytics on Employee Attrition

AI is used to analyze the reasons for low employee retention rates. Machine learning is used to create a prediction model to identify key factors behind an employee's decision to stay or leave an organization. The model achieved a 96% accuracy score and identified three main reasons for employee turnover. Explanatory AI methods were also used to analyze the factors contributing to employee turnover.
